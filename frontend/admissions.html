Admissions

Your Journey to Engineering Excellence Starts Here

Admission Process

1

Application

Fill out the online application form with your details. Ensure all information is accurate and complete.

2

Entrance Exam

Appear for the ILMEC Engineering Entrance Exam (IEEE) or submit valid JEE Main scores.

3

Counseling

Attend counseling session based on your entrance rank. Choose your preferred department.

4

Document Verification

Submit all required documents for verification at the admission office.

5

Fee Payment

Pay the course fee to confirm your admission and secure your seat.

Eligibility Criteria

🎓 B.Tech (Undergraduate)

  • 10+2 or equivalent examination passed with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics
  • Minimum 60% aggregate marks in PCM
  • Valid JEE Main score or appear for ILMEC Entrance Exam
  • Maximum age: 25 years (relaxation for SC/ST)

📚 M.Tech (Postgraduate)

  • B.Tech/B.E. in relevant discipline with minimum 60% marks
  • Valid GATE score (preferred)
  • Valid ILMEC PG Entrance score
  • Maximum age: 30 years (relaxation for SC/ST)
